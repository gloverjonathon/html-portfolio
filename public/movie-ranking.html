My 3 Favorites Movies

Inception

Another masterpiece in the Christopher Nolan collection, Inception blends the space between what is reality and what we are dreaming. In this psychological thriller Leonardo DiCaprio leads a team to delve in the sleeping mind of a mutli billionare and dare to change his perception on life at the cost of their own.

Spider-Man: Across The Spider-Verse

In the sequel installment to the Spider-Verse movies Miles Morales returns as a Spider-Man who longs for the companionship of his friends that he met along his journey. But when he reunites with those he thought that he would never see again, things take a turn that he could've never imagined.

Get Out

Jordan Peele makes his Directors debut in classic fashion as "Get Out" grabs hold of the audiences attention and subverts in from expectations. "Get Out" is a psychological thriller with twists and turns that resonates with audiences even today.
