Some of my favorite movies of all time!

Inception

Another classic in the Christopher Nolan collection that follows a thief named Cobb who navigates a dangerous world that manipulates one mind while they dream. In his final hesit he attempts to change the perpection of someone's reality in hopes that he will finally see his children once more. But when the ghost of his wife comes back to haunt himself and his team, he faces his most challenging adventure yet.

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

In this sequel to Into the Spider-Verse, Miles attempts to navigate his life as a teenager and his heroics as Spider-Man but is finding lonliness as he misses those who helped shape his journey along the way. But when one of those individuals from his past returns, what was once joy soon turns into anguish and horror as he finds out what it truly means to be Spider-Man.

Get Out

In Jordan Peele's director debut, we follow the journey of a couple as they take a weekend to visit his girlfriends parents. But when strange and random occurences keep adding up, Daniel finds himself deeper and deeper into the sunken place and he may not be able to get out.
